home building process
accessible to all through
AI and blockchain technology

Homeqube is a home-building application powered by artificial
intelligence (AI)that empowers the user to visualize his future
home.Through its built-in network of manufacturers, suppliers,
contractors, architects, and engineers, Homeqube helps future
homeowners optimize their design according to every budget,
taste, and lifestyle.

Long lead tIme

The traditional process of constructing homes can
be long, painful, and costly. The first step is to hire
professionals to help you conceptualize and design
your house. The next step is to apply for permits
from a building official. Only upon its issuance can
construction commence. The process for a typical
two-storey home, for example, takes about twelve
(12) months, without factoring in unforeseen events.

Homeqube can shorten lead time by using
artificial intelligence, prefabrication, and easier parts
assembly.
