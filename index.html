What is homeqube?

Homeqube is a home-building application powered by artificial intelligence (AI) that empowers the user to visualize his future home. Through its
built-in network of manufacturers, suppliers, contractors, architects, and engineers, Homeqube helps future homeowners optimize their design
according to every budget, taste, lifestyle, and more.

When using the app, Homeqube will guide you through the entire process of designing your home. This includes automatically plotting your lot
area through optical character recognition (OCR), designing your home using our “user-centric robust controls” (knobs), and eventually generating
essential documents you would need to build your house before move-in.

What makes the home design experience on Homeqube different is that anyone with access to the Internet can use it, whether or not you are in
the actual process of buying or building your own home. Information on cost, mobility, and dynamics is readily available. This gives users the
power to make informed decisions when it comes to life-changing commitments such as having a roof over their heads.

What’s more, users are incentivized to use the application through QUBE tokens and gamification. The more they engage, the more data will be
analyzed on the activities that provide the most value to all users. This will help the app grow, by creating more features that result in higher
engagement.

A decentralized online platform for home design such as Homeqube has never been done before because it also comes at the heels of global
problems that the world has never experienced before: a once-in-a-lifetime pandemic, the worsening and accelerating impacts of climate change,
and heightened cyber insecurity. Through this application, we envision a significant reduction in common architectural and engineering
inefficiencies such as material and labor waste while giving both the supplier and end-user access to useful information and resources in the
home building process.

Homeqube is being developed by a team of experts from multiple disciplines with years of experience in architecture, engineering,
manufacturing, and construction. One of our premises is that the main population of built architecture was built on top-down construction
processes, and therefore the aggregated aesthetic potentialities of thereof are limited and based thus. We want to introduce a new batch of
potential aesthetics for the next century living with AI in our midst.

Our mission is to make the home building processes accessible to all through AI and blockchain technologies to serve humanity’s aggregated
home requirements for long-term sustained success. Our vision is to provide a better Home Building Industry Environment Ecosystem through
our ‘socio- techno-economy” platforms that will create more justice, equity, liberty, for posterity.

Core Values: Above Board. Corporate Transparency. Love for God and Neighbor. Home-building For Everyone.

Long Lead Time

Traditionally, constructing homes can be long, painful, and costly. Hire professionals, apply for permits, and wait several months for a typical two-storey property.

Our difference
Shorter lead time using AI, prefabrication, and easier parts assembly

Lack of Technical Knowledge

Building and designing know-how stays with architects, engineers, and construction experts.


Our difference
A unique, sustainable, and fun way to build and design your home -- and earn tokens while at it!

No Direct Link to Manufacturers

Reaching out to suppliers, canvassing, and purchasing take time. Sometimes, materials that arrive at the site are either wrong, incomplete, or of subpar quality.

Our difference
Every part of your home connects to a recommended manufacturer, shortening the process and ensuring quality

No Linkage Between E-Commerce and House Design

Why can’t most users browse and canvass products remotely through online stores, like IKEA lets them?

Our difference
We provide users with information on specifications, brands, costs and more

High Carbon Emissions

Steel and concrete remain fundamental construction materials yet their production leads to a solid 4% of global CO2 emissions, accelerating the rate of climate change.

Our difference
Our parts and designs use special composites, which are proven eco-friendly, alternative structural materials

Heavy Reliance on Skilled Manual Labor

Tedious design computations and quality requirements, along with labor inefficiencies, just don’t cut it anymore, especially after COVID-19.
Our difference
Homeqube parts, dry and pre-engineered, have low skilled-assembly requirements. Building them out doesn’t require deep technical and construction skills.

Low Resilience Against Acts of Nature

Poor-quality houses are easy targets of strong winds, earthquakes, and severe weather events.

Our difference
Parts and assembly methods that are tested and proven to withstand extreme conditions

At the heart of the App is the “Knobs” to build the houses. These are based on deconstruction philosophies where different combinations of designs can be produced from the basic parts of the system. These Knobs are operated to modify the design of the house based on the users’ preferences. As you turn the Knobs you will see real-time implications in the cost of your home, lifestyle metrics, mobility metrics, and other value-precepts to guide you in the design decisions. As the App’s name implies, the massing of the home is based on cubes which represent a volumetric representation of a proposed system.

We intend to develop CI/CD of Knobs which we coin as “System Products” for the BE’s full optimization case uses which encompasses engineering, architecture, and construction multi stakeholder case uses. Our first Proof of Concept (POC) of the App, which will be available to the public, will have the first set of Knobs which can produce the first massing structure. The arrangement of the cubes will depend on these Knobs: namely the generation path Knob, Density Knob, and Complexity Knob. Users can have various arrangement combinations of the cubes which are constrained within the allowable buildable area and a maximum number of stories indicated in the input for your lot information.

As the app development progress, more knobs will be further developed. These knobs will represent algorithmic processes and design variables on different styles of massing, parts, mobility, design intent, enclosures, and other variables that affect home design. These knobs will expand the possibilities of home design by tweaking these variables that affect the design of homes.
