The People

Homeqube is a vision coming to life through an in-house team of thinkers, doers, and innovators on a collective journey to disrupt homebuilding pushing towards Industry 5.0 and beyond.


JP Calma
Founder / CEO / Director of Business & Enterprise Architect

JP is responsible for setting the overall technical direction and execution of the product or service strategy for the company. He was the CEO of Multi-Development & Construction Corporation (MDCC), the pioneer and founder of interior construction in the Philippines since 1975. His first encounter with the Built Environment was during his involvement in the family business, a highly specialized construction company catering to complex interior designs for the leisure and hospitality sector of the Philippines.

He has since founded a number of startups, namely Calma Properties, a real estate developer utilizing progressive, non-commercial, and above-standard design precepts, and C Ideation, which featured high-performance marine leisure architectural crafts made in the Philippines for the world. Through Homeqube, he has set his sights on launching an AI home building app for decentralized, deconstructed, democratized, and digital societies.

JP finished finance, entrepreneurial, and construction engineering research studies at the De La Salle University in 2002 and graduated from the Asian Institute of Management in the Philippines in 2004 and MIT Cambridge in the United States in 2006.


Max Aton, Jr.
Director of Service & Logistics / Partner

Max is a project construction management professional with 28 years of executive experience. He was involved in numerous projects ranging from residential and commercial buildings to huge engineering initiatives in areas such as hydropower plants. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of San Carlos in the Philippines in 1989 and a Master of Construction Management from the University of New South Wales in Sydney, Australia in 1992. Long years of expertise overseeing and troubleshooting projects across multiple disciplines have sharpened his management and industry know-how, enabling him to effectively synthesize theories in project implementation as well as proactively manage issues related to quality, cost, and time.

Alex Christopher Subijano
Director of Commercial & Legal Affairs / Partner

Alex is a practicing lawyer and a civil engineering graduate from San Beda University and Mapua University in Manila. His professional passions are focused on the disruption of traditional operative models on the Built Environment and synergizing the spheres of the legal industry, engineering education, and management through current emerging technologies. With his legal and technical backgrounds combined, Alex’s 15-year experience in both fields make him an expert and first mover in commercializing the industry into a new category of project delivery.

Gbadebo P. Rhodes-Vivour
Regional Advisory for Africa Region

Gbadebo is the creative director at Spatial Tectonics, Nigeria. His specialty includes alternative design and construction systems that increase affordability and access to affordable luxury. To this end, he has designed and worked with state governments on policy for modular buildings using repurposed containers and alternative construction materials. He is also the design lead for commercial and large-scale urban projects such as the Delta International commercial city.

Before founding Spatial Tectonics, he worked in Nigeria’s top architecture firms and Franklin Ellis architects in the United Kingdom. He also worked with the Chinese government on affordable housing design in anticipation of the Beijing Olympics as well as the American government in rebuilding New Orleans after Hurricane Katrina. He has a master’s degree in architecture from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in the US, and a bachelor’s degree from the University of Nottingham in the UK.

Donald Onghanseng
Chief Revenue & Compliance Officer (CRO & CFO)

Donald is a Certified Public Accountant and a practicing lawyer in the Philippines. He graduated cum laude from the University of the Philippines Diliman. He then pursued his law degree at the Ateneo Law School, where he graduated third highest in his batch and garnered the Dean’s Award for Best Thesis. He is also a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charter holder.

Donald’s practice and interests are focused on corporate, commercial, regulatory, data protection, and technology law with exposure to business finance management and structuring. He has worked with companies that have cross-border operations in several jurisdictions, where he directly advised executive directors and officers on financially sound business strategy decisions while mitigating risk in adherence to regulations. He has also had the opportunity to primarily handle investment funding negotiations, review investment definitive agreements, and manage due diligence matters in completing investment funding transactions.

Luis S. Silvestre Jr., Ph.D.
Director of Operating Systems (COS) / Partner

Luis obtained his Bachelor of Science in 2015, Master of Science in 2017, and Doctor of Philosophy in Mathematics in 2021 from Ateneo de Manila University in the Philippines. His research interests include mathematical physics and graph theories, information technology, and machine learning.


Sappy Saplala
Advisory on Information Technologies

Sappy is the Solution Engineering Director of the Philippine subsidiary of Oracle Corporation, a global leader in enterprise software spanning a portfolio of cloud, applications, software, and hardware. Prior to joining Oracle, he spent a fulfilling career in the telecommunications and financial services industries, giving him the credentials to be the trusted advisor of various C-level executives in business and technology. His engagements with organizations of various industries and sizes have allowed him to design several technology solutions that provide the benefits of digital transformation. Sappy takes pleasure in transforming visions into reality with the power of data and technology.

Kristoffer Dave Tabong
Director of Data Systems (CDO) / Partner

His eight years of experience in the civil engineering industry involves structural engineering design, BIM coordination, and project management. Kris graduated magna cum laude from the University of Santo Tomas and was a board topnotcher in civil engineering and master plumber. He earned a Master of Science in Data Science degree at the Asian Institute of Management. He recently earned his professional certification in Architecture and Systems Engineering at Massachusetts Institute of Technology
