Environmental, Social & Governance (ESG)

Our mission is to make the home building processes accessible to all, serving humanity’s aggregated requirements for long-term sustainability.

What We Do

The Homeqube app aims to provide a sound alternative to centuries-old home building practices by being at the crux of Web3.0 commerce.

Pioneering a decentralized online platform for home building with deep design integrations, we seek to address the worsening and accelerating impacts of climate change, alongside architectural and engineering process inefficiencies and a typically long, drawn-out process that can only bring about more shortage in the longer term.

Through tech-facilitated efficiencies, promoting alternatives to high-emission construction materials, as well as bridging the gap between customers and conscious industry providers, Homeqube advocates for sustainable home building.

Our Approach

When we design and build homes sustainably, we contribute to improving the environment and making it a better place for future generations.

Yet that’s not all. Sustainable building can lower the cost of running homes, saving water, energy, and maintenance on overall systems. It creates a healthy environment for families and bigger communities.

A sustainable home is an efficient property that’s built in a way that respects resources, optimizes energy, water, as well as material use, and lasts longer with high-quality systems.

Our ESG goals revolve around the following:

  • Help slow the accelerating rate of climate change through carbon emission reduction around home building processes, mechanisms, and market systems on the app
  • Enable customer success in our growing community, whether in their desired home design and construction goals, manufacturing success in our built-in marketplace, or the hygiene and security of their data within our app environment
  • Run smart, agile, well-governed business operations that respond to customer needs and deliver what is due to them

Sustainability, Technology, and the Supply Chain

Homeqube is cognizant of sustainability as one of the most important concerns among organizations across industries. Companies maintaining high ratings for ESG factors emerge in research as having a lower cost of debt and equity, outperforming the market in the medium and long term.

Supply chains greatly affect not just the environment but also business performance and society. Thus we have made it our mission to appropriately deploy new technologies to assist supply chains in creating and fostering a sustainable world.

We harness a combination of artificial intelligence (AI), blockchain, and the Internet of Things (IoT) to champion sustainability, in effect charting a path for the home building sector to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, reduce waste, optimize processes, mitigate long-present risks, and ensure efficiency in supply and transactions.

Blockchain technology, for instance, works to ensure the accuracy and transparency of data through the decentralized database that resists any form of tampering. Blockchain enables us to meet and present our ESG goals and standards through two key areas: data reporting and supply chain transparency.

As blockchain and similar technologies constantly expand, the possibilities in meeting and exceeding ESG goals through the Homeqube app and its operations across markets and territories are far-reaching.
